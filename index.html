Le Yang

I am currently a fourth-year Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Systems Engineering at City University of Hong Kong (CityU), fortunate to be advised by Prof. Siyang Gao. Before joining CityU, I obtained my master's degree from the Zhongtai Securities Institute for Financial Studies at Shandong University in 2020, fortunate to be advised by Prof. Zhen Wu. I obtained my bachelor's degree from the School of Mathematics at Shandong University in 2017. From May 2019 to August 2019, I interned at the Academy of Mathematics and Systems Science, Chinese Academy of Sciences, where I fortunately worked with Prof. Dacheng Yao. From January 2024 to June 2024, I visited at Department of Industrial and Systems Engineering, University of Southern California , where I fortunately worked with Prof. Sheldon Mark Ross.

I am interested in Theory of Machine Learning and Simulation Optimization. If you share common interests and would like to explore collaboration or have a discussion, feel free to contact me.
