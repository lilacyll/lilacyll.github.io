Biography

I work on multi-armed bandits and sequential decision making, with a focus on pure exploration and learning under uncertainty. My research aims to develop efficient algorithms with provable guarantees in complex and realistic settings. More broadly, I am interested in bringing together ideas from statistical inference, online learning, and simulation-based methods. In addition, I explore applications of bandit algorithms in a range of domains, including networking and data-driven decision-making problems such as insurance pricing.
